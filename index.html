Exploring Ethical Challenges in Mordern Computing

Scenario 1: AI-Generated Content

Your company, which provides data services to online content providers, is working with a new client managing lifestyle websites in cooking, travel, fitness, and health. The client, despite stable ad revenue, is replacing 40% of its image division with AI-generated content. Your team has been tasked with building an interface to integrate AI-generated images, which will be reviewed and approved by the remaining human staff. While the project is moving forward, concerns arise about the ethical and emotional toll of AI-generated content. A team member has abruptly quit, citing the difficulty of moderating such images, leaving you questioning the project's direction and the need to address potential legal, ethical, and human impact issues.

Option 1: Email to a Former Professor

Write to a former professor explaining the project and expressing your concerns about the ethical, legal, and emotional challenges of using AI-generated images. Highlight the derivative nature of AI content, potential copyright risks, and the impact on human reviewers. Discuss any unresolved questions you have and outline your thoughts on what actions you might take moving forward.

Option 2: Talk to Your Manager

Approach your manager with your concerns about the ethical implications of the project. Explain the risks for the client, your company, and public perception, emphasizing the emotional burden on reviewers and potential copyright issues. Advocate for a pause or adjustments to the project to address these challenges and ensure ethical standards are upheld.
